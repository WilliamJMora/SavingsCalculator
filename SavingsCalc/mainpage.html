Welcome to the Differential Equations Savings Calculator.

There are three types of calculators listed below:

1. Time to Reach Goal - this calculates the time it will take to reach a goal.

2. Deposit for Goal in Given Time - this calculates how much money to deposit on a weekly basis.

3. Amount After Given Time - this calculates the amount of money after a period of time.

Note: the calculators interpret the interest rate as compunded continuously or annually.
